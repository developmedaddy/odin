Oatmeal

Description

A true classic. Versatile, easy to make, satisfying, and incredibly nutritious.

Ingredients

  • oats
  • water and/or plant milk
  • salt
  • toppings (optional)

Steps

  1. Add the oats to a pre-heated pot and stir them constantly to toast them.
  2. Add the water or plant milk and continue stirring.
  3. When you achieve the consistency you like, transfer the oatmeal to a bowl.
  4. Top with cinnamon, peanut butter, fruit jam, seeds, or anything else you'd want!
