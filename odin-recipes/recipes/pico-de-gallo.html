Pico de Gallo

Description

Pico de gallo is a delicious mexican salsa originating from mexico. Although salsa translates as "sauce," pico de gallo is more like a salad. It is quick to make and requires only a few simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 10 ripe tomatoes
  • 3 onions
  • 2 jalapeño peppers
  • 1 lime
  • fresh cilantro
  • a pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Dice the tomato, onion, and jalapeño peppers and add them to a bowl.
  2. Finely chop the cilantro and add it too.
  3. Cut the lime in half and squeeze it into the bowl.
  4. Sprinkle in the salt, mix the ingredients and let the ingredients sit for 10 to 15 minutes - this helps the flavors to blend together
  5. Enjoy!
